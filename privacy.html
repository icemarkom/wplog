Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

Overview

wplog ("the App") is a water polo game log that runs entirely in your web browser. There is no server-side processing, no backend, and no data collection by the App. Your game data stays on your device.

Local Data Storage

Game data is stored in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Authentication

The App may offer sign-in through third-party authentication providers. Authentication is handled entirely by the respective provider. The App does not receive, store, or have access to your credentials. Sign-in is used solely to allow you to access your own App data on those services through the App.

Third-Party APIs

The App may use third-party APIs to read or write game data at your explicit direction — for example, exporting a game log to a spreadsheet in your own account. The App never accesses, retains, or shares this data independently. All interactions happen client-side, between your browser and the third-party service.

External Resources

The App loads fonts from Google Fonts for display purposes. The App may display advertisements from third-party advertising networks in the future. If advertising is introduced, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Analytics

The App may use anonymous, aggregate analytics to understand general usage patterns (for example, total number of users). No personally identifiable information is collected, stored, or shared through analytics.

Data Sharing

The App does not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party. The App has no server, no database, and no mechanism to access your data.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please open an issue on the wplog GitHub repository.
